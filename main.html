About

I'm a seventh-semester Informatics student at Universidad Autónoma de Sinaloa, Facultad de Informática, based in Culiacán, México. Outside the classroom I work as a freelance full-stack developer and penetration tester — building production React and Node applications during the day, and chasing vulnerabilities across bug bounty programs the rest of the time.

I hold the eJPT and CEHPC certifications, and I split my HackTheBox time between Active Directory attack chains and web exploitation labs. I think the two halves of what I do make each other better: building software teaches me where the weak points usually hide, and breaking software teaches me how to build it more carefully.
